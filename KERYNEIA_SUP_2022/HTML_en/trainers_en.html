Our philosophy

Being fully aware of the significant impact of sport and especially of Nautical Sports in balanced development of young people, in terms of health but also moral development.Our group envisions creating a big family athletes, to whom he will provide all the conditions and supplies for healthy occupation, which will protect them from threats of modern society.

In addition, our visions are of course for our athletes to maintain higher level of competition and to represent our country in major international events with the best performances, ever based on Fair Play.

The Kyreneia Nautical Club was founded in 1982 by a group of Kyrenia residents residing in Limassol, after their uprooting by the Turkish Invasion of 1974. This group felt the need to create a of national and competitive attraction that unites them all forces of the people of Kyreneia and to especially embrace the youth of Kyreneia.
