The Kyreneia Nautical Club today

The Kyreneia Nautical Club cultivates the sports of Swimming, of Canoeing - Kayaking, Sailing, Rowing and Triathlon. It has in its potential beyond 350 active male and female athletes , many of whom belong to her National Team Cyprus and represent it worthily in major sporting events abroad, such as the Olympic, World and Pan-European Games Championships, Commonwealth Games, University Games etc.
